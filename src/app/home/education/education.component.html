ISRN is implementing the Vedanta Group for the Anil Agarwal Foundation-supported project pertaining to the transformation of Anganwadi Centres into Nand Ghars. Operations & Maintenance of 275 Nand Ghars in Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan. We have also shared a proposal for 800 centres in Rajasthan for this FY 22-23.
Nand Ghar is committed to the Prime Minister's national vision of eradicating child malnutrition, providing education, and healthcare, and empowering women with skill development.
The project majorly focuses on four Verticals:

Nutrition

Pre School Education

Health Care

Skill Development
