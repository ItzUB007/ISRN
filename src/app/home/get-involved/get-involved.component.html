Internships

Selected intern's day-to-day responsibilities include:

  • Implementing Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects
  • Engaging in project planning
  • Performing primary and secondary research
  • Engaging in content development

Who Can Apply:

Only those candidates can apply who are available for full time (in-office) internship and are available for the duration of 3 months. The candidate should have relevant skills and interests. Women willing to start/restart their careers can also apply.

Perks:

Certificate, Letter of recommendation.
