Membership

Since we have been working with 800+ organisations in the far-flung pockets of the country, we are also welcoming new corporates, individuals, and grassroots organisations who want to explore our platform to connect with the unreachable.
Do you want to be a member? We will be happy to have you on our board. Click here to register! For more information or any queries, connect with us at info@isrn.in.

Benefits to Corporates

  • Project life cycle support from cradle to cradle
  • Expert advisory and facilitation for undertaking CS projects- Dovetailing business objectives with CSR mandate
  • Co-create CSR and social development projects with Government and grassroots organisations
  • Facilitate Public Private Partnership (PPP) in CSR
  • Quality Monitoring and Evaluation support for CSR projects
  • Access to data from credible and efficient grassroots organisations.
  • Participation in ISRN's events with a concessional fee- seminars, workshops, training, etc
  • Access to regular updates on CS and other critical information
  • Showcasing of CSR activities through website/promotional materials, newsletter, etc.
  • Improves relationships with the community and other stakeholders
  • Enhanced brand visibility and brand equity

Benefits to Grassroots organizations

  • Accreditation of grassroots organizations
  • Capacity building as per the needs in required domains
  • Interface with corporate donors
  • Project preparation at concessional rates
  • Organizational profiling for enhances visibility and impact
  • Enhanced visibility through listing on the ISRN website
  • Concessional participation in ISRN seminars, workshops, training, etc.
  • Handholding in the implementation of CSR projects
  • Access to regular updates on CSR, government programs, and opportunities
  • Provision of support services & facilitation to grassroots organizations at a nominal professional fee
  • Enhanced brand visibility and brand equity
