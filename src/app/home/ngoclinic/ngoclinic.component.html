NGO Clinic

We invite Subject Matter Experts from specific domains for an hour-long discussion followed by a Q & A session on the fourth Friday of each month.
Objectives:-

  • To understand the primary needs of the grassroots organisations and provide suitable need-based solutions
  • To generate ideas and supporting them with proper way of execution through utmost guidance from technical perspective
  • To create a bridge between the known and unknown facts of sustainability of the grassroots organisations
  • To build the capacity and create a knowledge enhancement platform for the grassroots organisations
  • Influence the inactive organisations to participate proactively
  • To brainstorm new ideas of innovation through a holistic development approach

Till date we have held 8 NGO Clinics Webinars under the themes:

  • Proposal Writing
  • Legal Compliances
  • Brand Building
  • Social Stock Exchange
  • Financial Inclusion of Sustainability of the Grassroots Organizations.
  • Sustainable Approaches of Community Development
  • Monitoring and Evaluation Framework for Sustainable Program Execution
  • Webinar on Clearing doubts about NGO Operations and Management
