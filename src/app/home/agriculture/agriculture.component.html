Agriculture

FPO Formation and

Promotion

ISRN has received 35 Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) for formation & management from Government agencies NAFED & SFAC in 4 states namely: Jammu & Kashmir, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and Himachal Pradesh. ISRN is also contributing in providing support to farmers in Credit & Market linkages, and Technical Trainings

Training and

Workshop

Several trainings and workshops were organised to support and promote 17 FPOs consisting of more than 5000 farmers. A technical training conference for farmers was organised on 9th January 2019 by PPRC in association with ISRN. The conference was inaugurated by Mr. Ashish Bhutani - IAS, CEO, PMFBY and Mrs. T.L. Alamelu, CMD, Agriculture Insurance of India who conducted the first session on Prime Minister Fasal Bima Yojna. The session was moderated by Shri Santosh Gupta , CEO ISRN.

Market

linkages

ISRN supported Farmers and FPOs in building effective market linkages to various big market FMCG companies and others such as Haldiram, Big basket, Grofers, DIF, Patanjali and Safal(Mother Dairy) etc. for their produce.
The aim of FPOs:

  • To Promote and support economically viable, democratically managed, and self-governed Farmer Producer Organisations.
  • To channel required assistance by way of policy, inputs, technical knowledge, market linkage, credit linkage, and infrastructure support to strengthen FPOs.
  • To remove hurdles in small & marginal farmers' access to markets through FPOs.
  • To create an enabling environment for fostering and nurturing FPOs.
