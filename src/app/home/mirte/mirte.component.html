COVID-19 Vaccination M-RITE Project!

The extensive work was carried out by the Indian Social Responsibility Network team in partnership with the John Snow India Private Limited (JSIPL) on the project supported by the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) to amplify the COVID-19 vaccination coverage in Nagaland and Meghalaya as part of the MOMENTUM Routine Immunization Transformation and Equity Project. The project demonstrated the importance of multi-stakeholder collaboration, evidence-based decision-making and effective communication strategies. As a result of the project, over 1.75+ lakh eligible beneficiaries were successfully vaccinated in Nagaland and Meghalaya. The Project involved a multi-disciplinary team that worked closely and collaborated with multiple departments, local health authorities, community leaders, volunteers and other influential stakeholders to identify and prioritize high-risk groups for vaccination.
A robust communication and outreach strategy was developed to increase vaccine awareness, dispel misinformation and build trust among hesitant communities. The public education and awareness campaign strategy involved educating the community on the importance of vaccination, addressing myths and misconceptions and encouraging vaccine uptake. The project reached out to vulnerable groups and implemented outreach campaigns to increase vaccine uptake in hard-to-reach areas.
