Indian Social Responsibility Network

The Indian Social Responsibility Network (ISRN) is a Not-For-Profit multi-dimensional facilitation network, established in July 2014, in the domain of Sustainable Development in India. With the goal of directing all essential authorities to provide services to the inaccessible regions of our society, we have established a results-oriented platform with specialists from all disciplines, sectors, and backgrounds. This shared platform is designed to bring together corporate, grassroots, and governmental organisations to develop a network that would ensure inclusive and sustainable growth and the greatest possible efficiency in reaching all elements of the community.

Vision

To create a sustainable network of Govt. institutions, Corporates, and Nonprofits in order to achieve the holistic development of the country.

Mission

Indian Social Responsibility Network would develop a platform for the integrated development of the unreachable with a multi-stakeholder approach to achieve a pathway for Sustainable Development Goals.

Goals

To create a network of Govt. institutions, CSR bodies, and grassroots organisations for serving the underserved

To work on different thematic areas of Education, Health, and Agriculture

To enhance the integrated capacity for the development of the grassroots organisations

Chairman

Anil Khaitan - Industrialist & Management expert

After attending Vincent High School in Mussoorie and completing his B.Com degree, Mr. Anil Khaitan, Chairman of SNK Corp., joined his family's businesses in 1976. In 1981, he completed his MBA from the International Management Institute in Geneva. Mr. Khaitan served as the former president of the Ph.D. Chamber of Commerce and Industry. He has established himself in the copper and steel, paper, and jute industries, among others. In addition to his extensive experience in several industries, he is a member of numerous National and International organisations, including the Advisory Council of the Indian Institute of Finance, AIMA, and the International Chamber of Commerce India.
